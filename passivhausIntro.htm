Passivhaus interface to PDT


Introduction

These pages provide your interface between Passivhaus, the building
simulator, and ProtoLife's Predictive Design Technology (PDT). This
interface allows you to explore an almost limitless set of possibilities
resulting in optimal values in your design---energy efficiency or costs.


The steps are:


1. Load your passivhaus file (to the left).


2. Define your variables for PDT to explore (second tab on the top, labeled 'Specify Variables'), one variable at a time.


3. Confirm variables, removing or adding variables, as necessary.


4. Launch PDT to find the sweet spot in the space of variables you have defined (third tab on the top, labeled 'Launch PDT').
